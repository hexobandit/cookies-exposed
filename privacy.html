Privacy Overview

Cookies Exposed is an educational website designed to help users understand browser cookies, their security implications, and privacy concerns. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

üîê Key Privacy Principle: All cookie analysis performed by our tools happens entirely in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your cookie data to our servers or any third parties.

Information We Collect

What We DO Collect

  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our educational content (page views, time spent, popular sections)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, and device information for compatibility purposes
  • User Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode) stored locally in your browser
  • Error Reports: Anonymous error logs to improve website functionality

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Cookie Data: Your browser cookies are never sent to our servers
  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Tracking Data: We do not create user profiles or track you across other websites
  • Sensitive Data: Passwords, session tokens, or authentication information
  • Location Data: Your physical location or IP address (beyond country-level for analytics)

Data Protection & Security

Technical Safeguards

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only what is necessary for basic functionality
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use tracking pixels, social media buttons, or advertising networks

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws

  • Right to Access: Request information about any data we may have about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (though we collect minimal data)
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for any data processing based on consent

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights or if you have privacy concerns:

  • Clear your browser data to remove any locally stored preferences
  • Use browser privacy settings to control analytics data
  • Contact us through the information provided in our About page
  • Use browser extensions to block analytics if desired
