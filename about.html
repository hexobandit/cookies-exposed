About Cookies Exposed

An educational project dedicated to raising awareness about browser cookie security and privacy implications.

Our Mission

Cookies Exposed exists to bridge the knowledge gap between everyday internet users and the complex world of browser cookies. While cookies are essential for modern web functionality, many users don't understand their security and privacy implications.

Our mission is to:

  • Educate users about what browser cookies are and how they work
  • Raise awareness of security risks like session hijacking, XSS, and CSRF attacks
  • Promote privacy consciousness regarding cross-site tracking and data collection
  • Provide practical tools for analyzing and understanding your own cookies
  • Empower users to make informed decisions about their online privacy
üí° Why This Matters: With increasing digital surveillance, data breaches, and privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA, understanding cookies is crucial for maintaining your online privacy and security.

Why We Built This

The Problem

Despite cookies being fundamental to web security and privacy, most users lack understanding of:

  • How cookies enable both functionality and tracking
  • The difference between session, persistent, and third-party cookies
  • Security attributes like Secure, HttpOnly, and SameSite flags
  • How attackers exploit cookies in various cyber attacks
  • What users can do to protect themselves

Our Solution

We created an interactive, educational platform that:

  • Explains complex concepts in accessible, non-technical language
  • Provides hands-on analysis of real cookies in your browser
  • Demonstrates security risks with practical examples
  • Offers actionable advice for improving privacy and security
  • Maintains complete privacy by processing everything locally

Technology & Privacy by Design

Technical Implementation

  • Client-Side Processing: All cookie analysis happens in your browser using JavaScript
  • No Backend Database: We don't store or collect your cookie data
  • Modern Web Standards: Built with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript
  • Security Headers: Implemented CSP, HSTS, and other security measures
  • Responsive Design: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Privacy by Design Principles

  • Proactive not Reactive: Privacy protection built into the system from the start
  • Privacy as the Default: No opt-out required - privacy is the default state
  • Full Functionality: Achieving education goals without compromising privacy
  • End-to-End Security: Comprehensive security measures throughout
  • Visibility and Transparency: Open about our practices and methods
üîí Privacy Guarantee: Your cookie data never leaves your device. Our scanner tool works entirely in your browser, ensuring your sensitive information remains private.

Important Limitations

Technical Limitations

  • JavaScript-Only Access: Our scanner can only see cookies accessible to JavaScript (not HttpOnly cookies)
  • Limited Cookie Attributes: Some cookie properties are not available through browser APIs
  • Browser Restrictions: Different browsers may limit what information is available
  • Cross-Origin Limitations: We can't analyze cookies from other domains

Educational Scope

  • General Education: This is educational content, not professional security advice
  • Rapidly Evolving Field: Web security constantly changes; information may become outdated
  • Browser Differences: Cookie behavior may vary between different browsers
  • Complexity Simplified: We simplify complex topics for accessibility
‚ö†Ô∏è Disclaimer: While we strive for accuracy, this tool is for educational purposes only. For critical security decisions, consult with cybersecurity professionals and refer to official browser and security documentation.
